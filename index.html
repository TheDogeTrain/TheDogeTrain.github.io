Recipe #1

  • Ingridients: 1. 8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni. 2. 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour. 3. 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese 4. 2 tablespoons butter 5. 1/2 cup bread crumbs 6. 1 pinch paprika. 7. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 8. 3 cups milk
  • Time:30 minues
  • Serves: 3-5 people
  • Best Mac N Cheese I've had
  • Doesn't take that long to cook compared to other Mac N Cheeses
  • The cheese is way better than any mac n cheese im my opinion
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Buy all ingridients.

Step 2: Cook macaroni according to the package directions. Drain.

Step 3: In a saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat. Stir in enough flour to make a roux. Add milk to roux slowly, stirring constantly. Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and the sauce is a little thick. Put macaroni in large casserole dish, and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir well.

Step 4: Melt butter or margarine in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and brown. Spread over the macaroni and cheese to cover. Sprinkle with a little paprika

Step 5: Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Serve

Step 6: Enjoy :D

Recipe #2

  • Ingridients: 1. 5 frozen Parker House I used Rhodes brand rolls, thawed. 2. 3 tablespoons sesame seeds 3. 1 pound ground beef. 4. 1 teaspoon salt. 5. 1 teaspoon pepper. 6. 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce. 7. 8-10 slices of bacon cooked 8. 10 cherry tomatoes sliced in half. 9. 5 lettuce leaves. 10. 5 deli slices sharp cheddar cheese
  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 3-5 people
  • The mini cheese burgers have so much flavor and texture
  • Doesn't actually take that long to cook considering how good they are
  • My favorite type of mini burgers to make
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Buy all ingridients.

Step 2: For the buns: Preheat oven to 350°F

Step 3: For the buns: Divide each thawed roll into four and roll into a round ball/disc shape. Place each mini roll on a parchment lined baking sheet and cover with plastic wrap. Allow to rise for 30 minutes.

Step 4: For the buns: Remove plastic wrap, and brush each ball of dough with a bit of melted butter and sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Step 5: Bake for 12 to 15 minutes or until golden brown.

Step 6: For the buns:Allow to cool before slicing in half.

Step 7: For the burger: Combine ground beef, salt, pepper, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix well and divide into 20 patties

Step 8: Preheat grill to medium and cook each burger for 2-3 minutes or until cooked through.

Step 9: Top each burger bun with a small piece of lettuce, cheese, small piece of bacon, and tomato!

Recipe #3

  • Ingrdients: 1. 1 package (1/4 ounce) active dry yeast. 2. 1 small onion, chopped. 3. 1 teaspoon sugar. 4. 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce. 5. 1-1/4 cups warm water (110° to 115°). 6. 3 teaspoons dried oregano. 7. 1/4 cup canola oil. 8. 1 teaspoon dried basil. 9. 3-1/2 cups all-purpose flour 10. 1 medium green pepper, diced. 1.. 2 cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese.
  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 3-5 people
  • The mini cheese burgers have so much flavor and texture
  • Doesn't actually take that long to cook considering how good they are
  • My favorite type of mini burgers to make
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Buy all ingridients.

Step 2: In large bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in water; let stand for 5 minutes. Add oil and salt. Stir in flour, a cup at a time, until a soft dough forms.

Step 3: Turn onto floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, about 2-3 minutes. Place in a greased bowl, turning once to grease the top. Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 45 minutes. Meanwhile, cook beef and onion over medium heat until no longer pink; drain

Step 4: Punch down dough; divide in half. Press each into a greased 12-in. pizza pan. Combine the tomato sauce, oregano and basil; spread over each crust. Top with beef mixture, green pepper and cheese.
